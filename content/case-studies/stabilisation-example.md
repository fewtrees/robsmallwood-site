## Context
Existing production workflow system with fragile implementation and chaotic delivery.

## Risk
Unmanaged technical debt and unclear delivery practices created operational risk and reduced confidence in delivery predictability.

## Intervention
- Established clearer ways of working across analysis, delivery, and operations  
- Triaged technical debt and introduced controlled remediation  
- Set a roadmap for architectural evolution aligned to deliverables  

## Outcome
Improved delivery predictability and reduced operational fragility, enabling the programme to proceed on a more sustainable basis.
