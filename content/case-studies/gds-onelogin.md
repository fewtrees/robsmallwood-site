## Context
Large-scale identity programme spanning multiple teams in a complex AWS environment.

## Risk
Delivery and operational risk increased as programme scale grew; leadership required clear visibility of technical risk and prioritisation.

## Intervention
- Provided programme-level technical assurance across multiple delivery teams  
- Reviewed architectural approaches and constraints (non-hands-on)  
- Identified and managed risks; supported incident reviews and remediation direction  
- Introduced clearer resilience and operational standards; improved health reporting  

## Outcome
Improved consistency of technical decision-making across teams, reduced risk through proactive governance, and strengthened resilience posture in production.
